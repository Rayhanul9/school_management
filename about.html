About KGHS:

Kurigram Govt High School is one the respected educational institute at College road area of the Kurigram District.
The School was started journey in 1895. It is a historical educational institute in Bangladesh.
It has been playing a significant role to develop the educational and culture of the district.



Facilities in the Kurigram Govt High School

1.Large campus covered by the beautiful natural elements.

2. Wonderful infrastructure of the school.

3. Green and large playground for students..

4. Adequate skilled and high educated teacher and trainer.

5. Large and modern Science Lab in the school.

6. Digital Lab for technology study and online class.

7. Cultural club for extra curriculum.

8. Scout team, BNCC team and trainer in the schools.

9. Innovation club in the school.

10. Sport and culture center in institute.


Requirement of the Kurigram Govt High School for Admission

1. Birth Certificate of the student

2. Passport size photograph

3. NOC from previous school or last educational certificate
